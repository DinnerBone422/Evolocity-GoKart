Update 2: Advancing the Build

Welding & Frame Construction

As we continue to refine our welding techniques, we’re getting closer to making a final decision on the frame. The choice between modifying a premade frame and building one from scratch is still on the table, but we’re making progress in evaluating the best option based on our project timeline and goals.

Powertrain Development

Our team has successfully powered up the motor and motor controller with temporary batteries, laying the groundwork for the integration of our custom-built battery pack. This initial setup allows us to start testing and making adjustments to ensure that our go-kart will perform at its best when we switch to the final battery configuration.

Website Development

We’ve also made strides in developing our project website, which will serve as a hub for sharing updates and documenting our journey. This site will be a key tool for keeping our supporters informed and engaged as we approach the competition.

Next Steps

Looking ahead, our focus remains on refining the powertrain and finalizing our frame choice. Stay tuned for more updates as we continue our exciting journey.

Update 1: Laying the Foundation

Welding & Frame Construction

We’ve begun one of the most critical aspects of our project: preparing the welding equipment and learning to weld with precision. After acquiring a high-quality TIG welder, we focused on mastering the necessary skills to construct a sturdy and lightweight frame. Our current task is deciding whether to modify a premade go-kart frame or build our own from scratch using aluminum. This decision is crucial, as it will determine how we allocate our time and resources moving forward.
