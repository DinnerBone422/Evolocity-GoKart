Welcome to Our EVolocity Journey!

We're a dedicated team of students working together to design, build, and race our own electric go-kart. Through the EVolocity program, we’re learning the ins and outs of engineering, teamwork, and sustainable technology—one lap at a time.
Our mission is to grow our skills, enjoy the process, and make the most of this incredible experience. Whether you’re here to follow our progress, support our efforts, or just share in the excitement, we’re glad to have you along for the ride!
