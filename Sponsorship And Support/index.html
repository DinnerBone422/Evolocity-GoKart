Our Sponsor’s Vital Role

A special thank you goes out to our generous sponsor, whose belief in our team has fueled our progress and made our journey possible. Their financial support has been instrumental in covering the costs of materials, parts, tools, and other essential expenses that allow us to bring our go-kart vision to life. How Our Sponsor Supports Us:

  • Financial Assistance: Providing the funds necessary to purchase materials, components, and tools required for the construction of our go-kart.
  • Encouragement: Offering us motivation and confidence through their belief in our abilities and potential.
Our sponsor’s contribution is not just about the financial aspect; it’s also about the trust and encouragement they’ve given us. We’re proud to have them as part of our team and look forward to making them proud with our achievements.

Support from Roncalli College

Our journey with EVolocity wouldn’t be possible without the unwavering support of our college. From providing us with the resources and facilities needed to build our go-kart, to offering guidance and encouragement every step of the way, our college has been the backbone of our project. How Our College Supports Us:

  • Facilities & Resources: Access to workshops, tools, and materials essential for constructing our go-kart.
  • Mentorship: Guidance from teachers and staff who help us navigate the technical and logistical challenges of the project.
  • Opportunities for Growth: Encouraging our participation in EVolocity to develop practical skills, teamwork, and leadership.
We’re incredibly grateful for this support, which not only enables us to take on this exciting challenge but also prepares us for future endeavours in the fields of engineering, technology, and sustainability.
