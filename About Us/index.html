Team Vision and Values

Vision

At the heart of our EVolocity journey is a shared vision: to learn, collaborate, and grow as a team while embracing the challenges and rewards of building an electric go-kart. We aim to create a project that reflects our skills, creativity, and commitment to sustainability and innovation. Our focus is on making the most of this experience, learning from every twist and turn on the track.

Our Values

Teamwork

We believe that the best outcomes come from working together. Each member of our team brings unique strengths, and by supporting one another, we achieve more than we could alone.

Learning

EVolocity is an opportunity to expand our knowledge in engineering, problem-solving, and collaboration. We value every lesson learned, knowing that it prepares us for the future.

Sustainability

We’re committed to understanding and applying sustainable practices in our project. Building an electric go-kart is just the beginning; we aim to foster a mindset that prioritises the environment in all we do.

Perseverance

Challenges are part of the journey, and we’re ready to face them head-on. We value resilience and the ability to keep going, even when the road gets tough.

Enjoyment

Above all, we believe in having fun with what we do. This project is not just about competition—it's about enjoying the process, celebrating our achievements, and making memories along the way.
